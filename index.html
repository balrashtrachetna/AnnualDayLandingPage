Sample Annual Day Themes

Scroll to explore our thematic concepts — each crafted for values, cultural depth, and easy execution.

Wari – The Devotional Tradition

Role plays of saints, soulful kirtan & bhajan, devotional dances and the Ringan Sohala — nurturing devotion, equality and unity.

Swarajya – The Rise of Shivaji Maharaj

A child-friendly retelling from birth to coronation using traditional songs, skits and dance — celebrating courage & leadership.

Festivals of India – Colors of Celebration

Skit, dance and traditional music performances highlighting Makar Sankranti, Diwali, Holi and more — showcasing unity in diversity.

Dashavatar – The Ten Incarnations

Age-appropriate acts inspired by divine forms representing truth, courage, kindness and wisdom — a visual story of goodness.

Tribute to Azadi – The Freedom Journey

An emotional tribute to India’s freedom, honoring unsung heroes through dramatizations, songs and dance — building patriotism and pride.

Untold Stories of Our Epics

Lesser-known moments retold through storytelling, music and symbolic play — teaching empathy, honesty and devotion to little hearts.
